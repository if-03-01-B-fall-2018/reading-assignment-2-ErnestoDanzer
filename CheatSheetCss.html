Css:

1. Css-rule: Consists of a selector, declaration block, name and value
2. Selectors: Descendant Selector = "div p", Child Selector = "div > p", Adjacent sibling selector = "div + p", General sibling selector = "div ~ p"
3. Colour specification: The higher the value of the hex notation is the brighter the colour.
4. Background image declaration: background-image: url(...), url(...);
5. Declaration text alignment: text-align: center, left, right, justify;
6. Declaration text-decoration: text-decoration: overline, line-through, underline, underline overline;
7. Declaration text-transform: text-transform: uppercase, lowercase, capitalize;
8. Serif = Thick and thin strokes, Sans-Serif = Strokes have even width
